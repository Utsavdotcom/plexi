How It Works

Your one-stop platform for connecting job providers with skilled taskers.

For Job Providers

1

Post a Job

Post a job for free, specify requirements and set your budget

2

Select a Tasker

Browse profiles, reviews, and previous tasks to choose the right person

3

Secure Payment

Payments are held until task completion, with review options available

For Job Seekers

1

Search for Jobs

Browse job requests and make counter offers

2

Send Proposals

Send proposals to job providers, showcasing your skills

3

Complete Jobs

Accept jobs and complete them to earn income

Users can rate and review taskers after completing tasks. This feedback is valuable for others considering assigning tasks to them later on.
